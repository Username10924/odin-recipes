Pizza

Description

Pizza is an italian dish that usually consists of cheese and tomato sauce

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to the recommended temperature for pizza baking (usually around 450-500°F or 230-260°C).
  2. Place your prepared pizza on a baking sheet or pizza stone and slide it into the preheated oven.
  3. Bake the pizza for approximately 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
